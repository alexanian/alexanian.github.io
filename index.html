My Deal

Lately I've been focused on steering towards nice futures for biotechnology.

This mostly happens through my work with the iGEM Foundation, where I help run the Responsibility program. We do a lot of things, but I'm most excited about our efforts to incentivise differential technology development, enable alumni to pursue biosecurity careers, and empirically investigate whether our interventions to build a "culture of responsibility" are working.
